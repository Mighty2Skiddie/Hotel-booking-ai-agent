# 🏨 HotelBot — AI Hotel Booking Assistant

Welcome to **HotelBot**, your intelligent hotel booking assistant powered by **LangGraph** and **OpenAI**!

## What I Can Do

| Feature | Example |
|---|---|
| 🔍 **Search Hotels** | *"Find hotels in Jaipur for Dec 10-13"* |
| 🛏️ **Check Availability** | *"What rooms are available at Maharani Palace?"* |
| 🏨 **Hotel Details** | *"Tell me about Royal Haveli's amenities"* |
| 💰 **Compare Prices** | *"Which is the cheapest hotel?"* |
| ❓ **Policies** | *"What's the cancellation policy?"* |

## How It Works

I understand natural language — just chat with me like you would with a travel agent. I'll remember your preferences throughout our conversation, so you don't need to repeat yourself!

> **Tip:** Start by telling me your destination city and travel dates.
